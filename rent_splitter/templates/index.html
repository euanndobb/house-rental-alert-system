Floor-plan Rent Splitter

Draw a box on each bedroom's dimensions and on the total area, then split the rent by room size.

Upload a floor plan to begin.

Bedroom mode (green): drag a box around each bedroom's dimension text — they are numbered 1, 2, 3, … in the order you draw them.
Total area mode (red): drag one box around the total floor-area figure.
Include a little margin so the units (the “m”, the “ft²”) are inside the box.
